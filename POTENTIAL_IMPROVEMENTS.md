## Questions for Client

Before implementing improvements, confirm:

1. **Budget**: What is the budget for additional features?
2. **Timeline**: When do you need specific features?
3. **Priority**: Which features are must-haves vs nice-to-haves?
4. **Resources**: Who will manage new features?
5. **Content**: Who will create content for new sections?
6. **Training**: Do staff need training on new features?
